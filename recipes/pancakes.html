Simple and Easy Pancakes

Breakfast is an important meal of the day and sometimes it can be difficult to squeeze in time to cook and eat in the morning. This Simple and Easy Pancake recipe will make you look like a pro at breakfast without using too much of your time.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
